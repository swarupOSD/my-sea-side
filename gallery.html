Why We Love Sea

A seascape is a view or depiction of the sea, often including the coastline and sky, or a work of art that represents this view. It can also refer to the marine environment itself, including its character and visual aspects. Furthermore, "seascape" can be used in the context of protected areas managed for conservation, or in scientific studies of spatial patterns in marine environments.
